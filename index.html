About Me

Hey there! I’m Poorva, a communication design student at Atlas University in Mumbai, where my journey into the world of design began. Ever since I can remember, I've been captivated by the power of visual storytelling. Whether doodling in my notebooks or experimenting with colors and shapes, I’ve always found joy in creating something that speaks to people.

Currently, I’m diving deep into the realms of graphic design, branding, and user experience. My passion lies in crafting designs that not only look good but also communicate ideas effectively. I believe that great design can bridge gaps and foster connections, and I strive to bring that philosophy into every project I undertake.

When I'm not buried in design projects or sketching ideas, you can find me exploring Mumbai’s vibrant streets for inspiration or indulging in my love for photography. Each experience fuels my creativity and helps me grow as a designer.

I invite you to connect with me and join me on this exciting journey of creativity and innovation. Let’s create something amazing together!
